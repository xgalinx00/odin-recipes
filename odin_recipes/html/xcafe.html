ABOUT X CAFE

X CAFE was found in 2015. The owners are special chefs that wanted to spend their retirement in a sweet small cafe not only making cofees but also making new friends!

According to the owners and the regular customers; the best order is an iced matcha latte with their speacial pumpkin cheesecake!
