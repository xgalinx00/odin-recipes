Special Pumpkin Cheesecake

Well the pumpkin spice time is at the door and what is better than a pumpkin latte? A Pumpkin Cheesecake! Well if love pumpkin and if you like cheesecake this special dish from The X Cafe is just for you! I can hear you already asking the question; "What's in it?"

Let's find out with the ingrediants list!

    Crust
  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs (from about 12 whole graham crackers) *might contain gluten!
  • 6 Tbsp (3/4 stick) melted, unsalted butter *contains dairy!
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
    • Filling
  • 3 (8-ounces each) packages cream cheese, room temperature *contains dairy!
  • 1 1/2 cups (about 9 oz by weight) packed light brown sugar
  • 15 oz canned pumpkin pie mix (use half of the 30 oz can)
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup sour cream *contains dairy!
  • 2 Tbsp all-purpose flour *contains gluten!
  • 2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 Tbsp real vanilla extract
